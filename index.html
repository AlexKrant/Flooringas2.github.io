Frequently asked questions

Read our full FAQ
How much does a flooring installation cost?

There is a wide range in what can be spent on a carpet, luxury vinyl plank, or hardwood floor project. Key factors to consider are the size or square footage of the job, the type of material, and whether you want us to prep your space for installation.

We offer free in-home estimates on our flooring installation costs and can give you further pricing guidance in our showroom.

Do you service businesses?

Yes, absolutely! Our high-quality craftsmanship extends to the wonderful businesses of Central Oregon. If you own a company here in town, you know the value and benefits of having a space that feels comfortable and welcoming.

New floors can go a long way to maintain customers, providing an environment that looks and feels professional, so connect with us to learn more about how we can help.

Where do you install hardwood floors?

If you 've got a room, we 've got a floor for it! From living rooms and kitchens, to bathrooms, basements, bedrooms, and everywhere else.

Every client 's needs vary from one to another, so depending on your floor installation desires we can get you squared away with whatever you 're looking for.
